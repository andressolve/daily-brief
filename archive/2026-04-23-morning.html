1. Third US seizure breaks the mirror equilibrium. The DoD released video of forces on deck. The tit-for-tat was at 2-for-2 (US: Touska + Tifani; IRGC: MSC Francesca + Epaminodes). Now it’s 3-2. The US is deliberately escalating the seizure game. 2. Trump’s “shoot and kill” order is ROE-level public language. Via Truth Social: “I have ordered the US Navy to shoot and kill any boat that is laying mines in the Strait of Hormuz.” He also declared “total control” of the Strait and said it is “Sealed up Tight, until such time as Iran is able to make a DEAL!!!” Tripled minesweeper operations. 3. Iran toll revenue = institutional lock-in. Deputy speaker Hajibabaei confirmed the first Hormuz toll payment was deposited into the Central Bank. Amount undisclosed; tolls vary by cargo type, volume, and risk level. This is the structural story the market hasn’t fully priced. Once Iran collects revenue from Hormuz tolls, there is a bureaucratic and financial constituency for maintaining the toll regime. Closing the Strait is no longer just a military tactic — it’s a revenue stream. Reopening now requires Iran to give up income, which is politically harder than lifting a military blockade.
